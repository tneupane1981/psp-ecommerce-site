Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about Pet Supply Plus.

📦 Ordering

Placing an order is easy! Browse our products, add items to your cart, then proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide your shipping address, select a shipping method, and enter your payment information. Once your order is placed, you'll receive a confirmation email with your order number.

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement, before they enter our fulfillment queue. To make changes, contact our customer service team at 1-800-PET-PLUS or use our live chat. Once an order has been processed or shipped, modifications are not possible.

No, you can check out as a guest without creating an account. However, creating an account allows you to track orders, save your address for faster checkout, view order history, and receive exclusive member discounts.

Promo codes can be entered in the "Order Summary" section of your shopping cart. Click the "Apply Code" button after entering your code. Only one promo code can be applied per order. Codes cannot be combined with other promotions unless specifically stated.

🚚 Shipping

We offer three shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping (5–7 business days): FREE on orders over $75; $8.99 on orders under $75
  • Express Shipping (2–3 business days): $9.99
  • Overnight Shipping (next business day by 12pm): $24.99

Orders placed before 2pm EST on business days ship the same day.

You can track your order using our Order Status page. Enter your order number and the email address used at checkout. You'll also receive tracking information via email once your order ships.

Currently, Pet Supply Plus ships to all 50 U.S. states and U.S. territories (Puerto Rico, Guam, U.S. Virgin Islands). We do not offer international shipping at this time, but we are working on expanding our shipping coverage. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when international shipping becomes available.

🔄 Returns & Exchanges

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and accompanied by the original receipt or order confirmation. To initiate a return, contact our customer service team and we'll provide a prepaid return label. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days of receiving the returned item.

Yes! Exchanges are available for most products within 30 days of purchase. Contact our customer service team via live chat or phone with your order number and the item you'd like to exchange for. We'll arrange a free exchange with no additional shipping charges.

For health and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Opened pet food or treats
  • Live animals
  • Prescription medications or health supplements after opening
  • Custom or personalized items
  • Items damaged by misuse or normal wear and tear

If you receive a defective product, please contact us within 30 days for a replacement or refund.

🐾 Products

Choosing the right pet food depends on your pet's species, age, weight, and any health conditions. We recommend consulting with your veterinarian for specific dietary needs. Generally, look for foods with a named protein as the first ingredient, AAFCO complete and balanced statement, and appropriate life stage labeling. Our customer service team includes certified pet nutritionists who can provide free guidance.

👤 Account

To reset your password, click "My Account" in the top navigation and select "Forgot Password." Enter your registered email address and we'll send you a password reset link within a few minutes. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact our customer service team for assistance.

You can unsubscribe from our marketing emails at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email we send you, or by updating your communication preferences in your account settings. Please allow up to 10 business days for the change to take effect. You will still receive transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping notifications) that are necessary for your purchases.
