Turtle Habitat Kit

$79.99

Create the perfect aquatic environment for your turtle with our complete Turtle Habitat Kit. This all-in-one 20-gallon setup is designed specifically for red-eared sliders, painted turtles, and similar aquatic turtles. Included is a clear 20-gallon glass tank, an adjustable elevated basking platform with ramp, a 150 GPH submersible power filter, a 13W compact UVB fluorescent lamp with fixture, a digital thermometer, water conditioner, and a feeding dish. Everything your turtle needs to stay healthy, active, and happy.
