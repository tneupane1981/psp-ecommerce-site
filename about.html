About Pet Supply Plus

Serving pet owners with passion and expertise since 2010.

Our Story

Our Mission

To provide pet owners with premium quality supplies, expert advice, and outstanding service — because every pet deserves the best.

Pet Supply Plus was founded in 2010 by husband-and-wife team Robert and Patricia Chen, passionate animal lovers who saw a gap in the market for high-quality, affordable pet supplies. What began as a small brick-and-mortar store in Columbus, Ohio has grown into a nationwide e-commerce destination serving over 500,000 happy pet owners.

Over the past 15 years, we've expanded our product catalog from just 200 items to more than 10,000 products covering dogs, cats, birds, fish, reptiles, and small animals. We partner with over 150 trusted brands and manufacturers to ensure every product meets our rigorous quality standards.

Today, Pet Supply Plus operates three physical retail locations and ships to all 50 states from our central fulfillment center in Columbus, Ohio. We remain committed to our founding principles: quality, affordability, and putting pets first.
