About Pet Supply Plus

Serving pet owners with passion since 2005

Our Story

Pet Supply Plus was founded in 2005 by animal lover Angela Roberts with a simple mission: to provide pet owners with high-quality supplies at affordable prices. What started as a small storefront in Anytown, USA has grown into a thriving multi-location retailer and online shop serving thousands of customers nationwide.

With over 20 years of experience in the pet supply industry, we understand what pets and their owners need. Our team of passionate animal lovers works tirelessly to curate the best selection of food, toys, accessories, and healthcare products for dogs, cats, birds, fish, reptiles, and small animals.

Our mission is simple: "Everything Your Pet Needs" — delivered with expertise, care, and a genuine love for animals. We believe every pet deserves the best, and every owner deserves a trusted partner for their pet care journey.
