Aquarium Starter Kit (20 Gallon)

$129.99

Start your aquarium hobby right with our complete 20-Gallon Aquarium Starter Kit. Everything you need is included in one box: a clear glass 20-gallon tank, a quiet-running hang-on-back power filter, a full-spectrum LED hood light, an adjustable 100W submersible heater, a digital thermometer, water conditioner, a fish net, and a substrate sample. Suitable for freshwater tropical fish, cichlids, goldfish, and planted tanks. The LED light features multiple color modes to showcase the natural beauty of your fish.
