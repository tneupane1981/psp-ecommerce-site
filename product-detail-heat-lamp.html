Reptile Heat Lamp

$34.99

Provide your reptile with the essential warmth and UVA light they need to thrive. Our Reptile Heat Lamp includes a 100-watt basking bulb and a 5.5-inch aluminum dome with a ceramic socket rated for bulbs up to 150W. The deep dome reflector maximizes heat output and directs it precisely into the basking zone. Compatible with all standard basking, halogen, and mercury vapor bulbs. Suitable for bearded dragons, iguanas, geckos, tortoises, and all basking reptiles.
