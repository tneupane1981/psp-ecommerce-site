Parrot Perch Set

$29.99

Keep your parrot's feet healthy and exercised with our Natural Wood Parrot Perch Set. This set of three perches is crafted from untreated, bird-safe natural wood in varying diameters and textures — mimicking the variety of branches found in the wild. Regular variation in perch diameter is essential for preventing arthritis and bumblefoot in parrots and other large birds. Each perch attaches securely to cage bars with threaded bolt hardware included. Set includes small (0.75"), medium (1.25"), and large (1.75") diameter perches.
