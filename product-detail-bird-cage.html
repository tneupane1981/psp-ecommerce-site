Deluxe Bird Cage

$89.99

Give your feathered friends a spacious and comfortable home with our Deluxe Bird Cage. Crafted from non-toxic powder-coated steel, this generously sized cage features two large access doors, four wooden perches of varying diameter, two removable stainless steel food dishes, a play-swing, and a pull-out bottom tray for easy cleaning. The wide bar spacing and sturdy construction make it ideal for parakeets, cockatiels, conures, and similar medium birds.
