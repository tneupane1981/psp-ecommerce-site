Cat Scratching Post Tower

Give your cat the ultimate play and rest space with our 5-foot Cat Scratching Post Tower. This multi-level cat tree features four wrapped sisal scratching posts that protect your furniture, three comfortable plush-covered perches, a cozy enclosed condo hideaway, and two hanging toy balls for bat play. The sturdy engineered wood base provides stability even for active cats. Assembly is tool-free with color-coded parts and clear instructions. Suitable for single or multiple cats up to 25 lbs each.
