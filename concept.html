Conceptual Site Overview

An overview of the vision, goals, and design philosophy behind Pet Supply Plus.

Purpose

Pet Supply Plus is a full-featured e-commerce website designed to provide pet owners with a convenient, trustworthy, and enjoyable online shopping experience. The site offers a wide selection of premium pet supplies covering dogs, cats, birds, fish, reptiles, and small animals — all in one place.

The primary purpose is to replicate the feel of a professional retail pet supply store online, complete with product browsing, a shopping cart, checkout flow, customer service, and informational pages.

Target Audience

  • Pet Owners: Individuals who own one or more pets and regularly purchase food, accessories, and care products.
  • New Pet Parents: First-time pet owners looking for guidance, starter kits, and essential supplies.
  • Gift Shoppers: People shopping for pet-related gifts for friends and family.
  • Veterinary Clients: Customers referred by vets seeking specific health or dietary products.

The site is designed to be accessible and intuitive for users of all technical skill levels, with clear navigation and accessible design.

Goals

  • Provide a seamless, end-to-end shopping experience from product discovery to order confirmation.
  • Build customer trust through transparent policies, clear product information, and excellent customer service.
  • Demonstrate modern web development best practices including semantic HTML, responsive CSS, and accessible design (WCAG 2.1).
  • Offer a scalable page structure that could be connected to a real back-end or CMS in a production environment.
  • Ensure fast load times and mobile-friendly layouts across all devices.

Site Structure

The site is organised into the following functional areas:

  • Main Pages: Home, About, Contact, Sitemap, Concept, Wireframes
  • Shopping: Products listing, individual product detail pages (11 products across 6 pet categories)
  • Cart & Checkout: Shopping cart, checkout form, payment page, order status / tracking
  • Customer Service: Live chat support page, FAQ / Help Center, order tracking
  • Account: Customer account dashboard
  • Legal & Policies: Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions

All pages share a consistent header, navigation, breadcrumb trail, and footer to ensure familiarity and ease of use.
